Treatibles is a premium WordPress WooCommerce store offering CBD‑based wellness products designed to support pet health and wellbeing.

The site was developed to support product discovery, educational content, and subscription purchasing while maintaining a clean and trustworthy shopping experience.

Key development work included:
• WooCommerce store setup and customization
• Structured product catalog for pet wellness products
• Subscription and repeat purchase functionality
• Educational content integration to build trust
• Performance optimization for eCommerce conversion

The platform supports both product sales and brand education, encouraging long‑term customer relationships.
